What's New in This Version:

1.8.1

* Default file extension is always added when saving. This is important
  for files with double extension like ".bol.fil".

1.8

* Support for Lo-Res (40x48, 16 colors) and Double Hi-Res (140x192, 16
  colors) Apple ][ graphics modes.
* NTSC TV-set emulation for all Apple modes.
* Ability to convert images between ZX-Spectrum (interleaved) and Picler
  (sequential) formats.
* Automatically guess format of images loaded from FIL containers.
* Keep image format and settings when switching from one image to
  another in the same format.
